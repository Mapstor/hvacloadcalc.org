## Summary

hvacloadcalc.org is an educational HVAC reference site funded by display advertising served through **Google AdSense** and its third-party advertising partners. This policy explains, in detail and without hedging, what data is collected, what cookies are set, what controls you have, and how to exercise your privacy rights.

The site collects only what is operationally necessary plus what advertising vendors set on it through standard ad-network integration. There is no customer database, no marketing email list, no behavioral profile, and no sale of personal information. If you want to use the site with no third-party cookies at all, block third-party cookies in your browser; every calculator and every article remains fully functional.

### Advertising and measurement data (Google AdSense and partners)

When you load a page on this site, Google AdSense and its third-party ad partners may set and read cookies and similar identifiers on your browser to deliver and measure advertising. ### Email correspondence

When you choose to email **info@hvacloadcalc.org**, the message and the email address it was sent from are received and read by the editorial inbox. This data is used solely to respond to your inquiry. It is not added to a mailing list, a marketing system, or any third-party CRM.

Correction reports may be referenced in the public [corrections log](/corrections/) — but reporter attribution there is opt-in only (the default is anonymous; you have to explicitly request attribution to receive it).

### Personalized versus non-personalized advertising

**Personalized advertising** uses information about your prior visits, inferred interests, and similar signals to select which ads to show you. **Non-personalized advertising** uses only contextual signals (the page content, your approximate location at country level, and your device type) to select ads, with no profile-based targeting.

EU, UK, and Swiss users are shown a Google-certified consent management platform on first visit and may choose personalized or non-personalized advertising. Users in other jurisdictions can switch to non-personalized advertising at any time via Google Ads Settings.

### EU/UK consent (GDPR and ePrivacy)

For users in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and Switzerland, the site complies with Google's EU User Consent Policy. A Google-certified consent management platform is presented on first visit. Until valid consent is recorded for personalized advertising, only non-personalized (contextual) advertising is served to those users. The consent state is recorded in a consent cookie or similar storage and can be revisited at any time through the CMP control.

### California residents (CCPA / CPRA)

For California residents, the California Consumer Privacy Act and the California Privacy Rights Act provide specific rights. hvacloadcalc.org does not sell personal information for monetary consideration and does not knowingly share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising in a way that requires affirmative opt-out under California law.

The Google AdSense integration may transmit identifiers to Google and its ad partners as described above; California residents who wish to opt out of personalized advertising should use Google Ads Settings or the DAA opt-out tool linked above. To exercise other CCPA rights (access, deletion, correction), email **info@hvacloadcalc.org** with subject "Privacy."

## How We Use Information

The categories of data described above are used as follows.

(1) **Server logs** operate and secure the site — serving requests, detecting abuse (excessive request rates, scraping attempts, malformed payloads), and diagnosing errors. They are not used for marketing, personalization, or behavioral profiling.

(2) **Advertising data** is used by Google and its third-party ad partners to deliver and measure ads, in line with Google's policies. The site itself does not access, retain, or process the advertising data Google collects — the integration is the standard AdSense embed.

(3) **Email correspondence** is used solely to respond to your inquiry. Correction reports may be referenced in the public [corrections log](/corrections/) when a correction is published; reporter identity is opt-in (the default is anonymous attribution).

(4) **No data is sold.** No data is shared with third parties for marketing-list resale or similar purposes. No "look-alike audiences" are built. The site does not have anything to sell because it does not collect anything to monetize beyond what Google AdSense receives through standard ad serving.

## GDPR, CCPA, and Equivalent Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have statutory rights including:

- **Right to access** the information held about you.
- **Right to correction** of inaccurate information.
- **Right to deletion** of your information.
- **Right to portability** of your information in a structured format.
- **Right to object** to certain processing.
- **Right to opt out of sale or sharing** of personal information.
- **Right to non-discrimination** for exercising privacy rights.

Because the editorial side of the site collects very little data (only what you submit by email, plus operational server logs), most editorial-side requests resolve quickly. Advertising-side data is held by Google and partners; the appropriate path for those requests is Google Ads Settings, My Ad Center, or a direct request to Google.

To exercise a right on the editorial side, email **info@hvacloadcalc.org** with subject **"Privacy"** and describe the request. Responses are provided within 30 days of receipt.

EEA, UK, and Swiss residents have GDPR rights. California residents have CCPA/CPRA rights. Brazilian residents have LGPD rights. Canadian residents have PIPEDA rights. All such requests receive the same response standard regardless of jurisdiction.

## Children's Privacy (COPPA)

This site is not directed at children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

The content covers HVAC sizing, building science, and equipment selection — topics aimed at adult homeowners. There is no account system, no form requiring personal information beyond email correspondence (which presumes adult or parental consent), and no targeting of children.

If you believe a child under 13 has provided information by emailing the site, email **info@hvacloadcalc.org** with subject "Privacy" and the information will be deleted.

Per Google's policies, advertising on this site does not target children under 13 with personalized advertising.

## International Data Transfers

Vercel and Google operate globally. When you load this site, data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than the one you are in, including the United States. Vercel and Google use standard contractual clauses, data privacy framework certifications, and other lawful mechanisms to govern those transfers.

For EU, UK, and Swiss users specifically: Google is certified under the EU-US Data Privacy Framework, the UK Extension to the DPF, and the Swiss-US DPF. The standard contractual clauses approved by the European Commission also apply to transfers as a fallback safeguard.

## Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated to reflect changes in site practice, applicable law, or third-party vendor disclosures. Material changes are noted with an updated "Last Reviewed" date at the top of the page and a summary entry on the [corrections log](/corrections/).

The Last Reviewed date indicates when the policy was most recently verified against actual site practice. If you see a Last Reviewed date older than three months, you can request an interim verification by emailing **info@hvacloadcalc.org**.

## Contact for Privacy Matters

For any privacy-related inquiry — exercising your rights, asking about specific practices, reporting a concern, or anything else — email **info@hvacloadcalc.org** with subject **"Privacy"** and describe the matter.

Responses to privacy inquiries are acknowledged within 7 days and substantively addressed within 30 days. There is no other privacy contact channel; the editorial inbox processes all privacy requests directly.
