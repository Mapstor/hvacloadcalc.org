# Manual S Residential Equipment Selection

ACCA Manual S is the methodology for matching equipment to the load Manual J calculated. The principle is straightforward: equipment performance varies with outdoor and indoor conditions, so the capacity stamped on the unit at AHRI rating conditions isn't necessarily the capacity available at your local design temperature. Manual S walks through the corrections.

This section covers the AHRI matchup process, sensible versus latent capacity, sensible heat ratio (SHR), the implications for variable-capacity equipment, and how cold-climate heat pump selection differs from conventional AC selection.

## What this section covers

- **AHRI matchup** — verifying the outdoor and indoor unit combination
- **Sensible vs latent capacity** — sizing for the dehumidification you actually need
- **Sensible heat ratio (SHR)** — what 0.75 means in humid vs dry climates
- **Variable-capacity equipment** — modulation range and selection
- **Cold-climate heat pump selection** — capacity at 17°F and 5°F
