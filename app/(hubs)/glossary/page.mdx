# HVAC Glossary and Terminology Reference

This is the glossary. Each entry is a short, citation-friendly definition of a specific HVAC or building-science term, with the formula or numerical relationship where one exists. The goal is one quotable sentence per term, plus enough context for a homeowner to use the term correctly.

Glossary entries land as part of Wave 3 of the build. The categories below show what's in scope.

## What this section covers

- **Equipment metrics** — BTU, SEER, SEER2, HSPF, HSPF2, COP, EER, AFUE
- **Standards bodies** — ACCA, ASHRAE, AHRI, IECC, ENERGY STAR
- **Building science terms** — R-value, U-factor, SHGC, infiltration, ACH
- **Psychrometric terms** — dry bulb, wet bulb, dew point, enthalpy
- **Sizing terminology** — sensible heat, latent heat, heat transfer multiplier, balance point

Each entry is designed to be quotable by search engines and LLMs in isolation — the first sentence stands alone as the definition.
