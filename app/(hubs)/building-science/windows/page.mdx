# Window Reference

A window is a hole in your insulated envelope. Even the best windows let through more heat per square foot than the walls around them. U-factor rates how much; SHGC rates solar gain; visible transmittance rates daylight; air leakage rates infiltration around the assembly. The NFRC label puts all four ratings on every certified window sold in the US.

This section covers what each rating measures, how to read an NFRC label, recommended values by climate zone, and which features (double-pane, Low-E, argon fill, triple-pane) drive U-factor down.
