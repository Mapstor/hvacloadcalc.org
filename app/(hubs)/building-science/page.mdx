# Building Science Reference for HVAC Sizing

HVAC sizing decisions are downstream of the building. R-values, U-factors, air leakage rate, climate zone, and design temperatures all flow into the Manual J load calculation. Treat the envelope as a black box and the sizing math has nowhere to anchor. Treat it transparently and the load calc becomes auditable.

This section covers the building science fundamentals that drive HVAC sizing: assembly R-values by climate, fenestration U-factors and SHGC, psychrometric properties, infiltration measurement, climate zones, and the design temperatures that anchor any load calculation.
