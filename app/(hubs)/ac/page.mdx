# Air Conditioner Sizing and Reference

Air conditioner sizing is more often wrong than right. The 30-second method most contractors use — square footage times a number — overshoots in well-insulated homes and undershoots in poorly-sealed ones. The result is either an oversized unit that short-cycles, dehumidifies poorly, and shortens its own life, or an undersized one that runs constantly without keeping up.

This section covers correct sizing methodology, the BTU-per-square-foot ranges by climate, the diagnostic patterns for short cycling and undersizing, and the practical steps a homeowner can take to verify a contractor quote.
